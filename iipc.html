INDUSTRY INSTITUTE PARTNERSHIP CELL

INDUSTRY INSTITUTE PARTNERSHIP CELL (IIPC) is a central facility established at Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Technology (MGIT). This cell is funded by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).

The aims of IIPC - MGIT are:

  • To establish a platform for enhancing relationship between MGIT and the Industries.
  • To promote the synergetic partnership between the Institute and Industry to exchange the expertise of the experts from the Industry and the Institute.

IIPC-MGIT has an advisory board with eminent people from Industries, R&D organizations, CII, Industry Associations, Entrepreneurs etc. The advisory board has Dr.K.Sudhakar Reddy, Principal, MGIT as Chairman, Dr.P.Venkata Ramana, Professor, Mechanical Engineering as Chief Coordinator
