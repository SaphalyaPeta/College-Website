The Placements of the institute has been on the rise since 2005-06.Our Graduates pursue Post Graduate and Research Studies in several institutions of repute within and outside the country. The Placement wing in the college is successful in providing placements to the out-going students through campus recruitment conducted by many organization of national and international repute.

The placement officer notifies the names of the companies that visit for placements to the students through circulars.
