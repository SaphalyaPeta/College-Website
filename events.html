The college is agog with activity all year long. Each department organizes Technical Fests,Expert Lectures and other Academic acitivities in the spirit of healthy competition. The College Day, "NIRVANA" is an enthralling occasion drawing crowds and participation froma all over the city. On this occasion academic, sports and cultural student achievers are awarded medals and certificates in recognition of their performance. Luminaries from the industry are invited to inspire the students.

Blood donation camps,tree planting and service to orphanages and old age homes coordinated by the NSS wing also draw enthusiastic participation from the students.
